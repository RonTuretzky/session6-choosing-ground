# Session 6 — Choosing Ground

An interactive reveal.js slide deck for the course *Design an Agrarian Industrial Commune* (The Convent, Greenpoint, Brooklyn).

Legal structures, revenue models, and — above all — **where in the world a commune actually pencils out**: the trade-off between quality of life, cost of land, and immigration difficulty, scored across **50 countries** on a six-lens rubric (**LAND · VISA · TAX · LAW · LIFE · STAB**).

Features:
- an interactive **three.js globe** (colour-blind-safe scoring, click any country for a full card)
- a **priorities questionnaire** merged into the globe that re-ranks jurisdictions live as you move the sliders
- per-country detail cards with land prices, tax, zoning, healthcare, pensions, and **visa specifics** (thresholds, durations, processing time, path to permanent residency)
- archetype deep-dives, an interactive plan-builder, per-slide notes with **copy-all**, and homework

**Live:** https://ronturetzky.github.io/session6-choosing-ground/

Single static `index.html` — open it directly or serve it over HTTP.

## Present

- `→` / `←` / space — navigate · `o` — overview · `f` — fullscreen · `s` — speaker notes
- `c` — toggle the per-slide comment bar (auto-saved to `localStorage`); the closing slide's **Copy all comments** exports a Markdown dump

## Data

A July 2026 synthesis of national tax / immigration / land-law sources (Global Citizen Solutions, PwC & KPMG tax summaries, Eurostat & national ag-land indices, migration authorities, Numbeo, Global Peace Index) plus course research. **Decision-support, not legal advice** — verify with a local attorney before acting.
